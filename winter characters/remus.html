Remus the Reindeer

Clumsy, well-meaning, and full of innocent chaos, Remus somehow manages to trip over snowdrifts, his own hooves, and occasionally thin air. He approaches the world with wide eyed wonder and wholehearted enthusiasm, often causing accidental mayhem but never harm. Remus reminds everyone that mistakes are part of the adventure - and that joy doesn't need to be graceful.

Favourite Hobby

Remus loves building snowy obstacle courses to see if he can make it through without tripping (he hasn't succeeded yet).

Favourite Snack

Cinnamon Gingerbread Biscuits
