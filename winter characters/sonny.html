Sonny the Squirrel

A lovable whirlwind of motion, Sonny seems to defy gravity on a daily basis - leaping, spinning, and appearing in places no one expects. He's the clown of the group, always ready with a distraction, a daring stunt, or a ridiculous idea when spirits are low. When things are heavy, Sonny is the first to lighten the mood, even if it means falling out of a tree to do so. To sum up Sonny... he's nuts.

Favourite Hobby

Sonny loves to bury his friend's prized posessions for a laugh.
