Promise the Polar Bear

Calm, kind and dependable, Promise embodies warmth even in the coldest places. He's gentle, reassuring, and always there when others need comfort. Being near promise feels like everything will be okay.

Favourite Hobby

Promise loves watching the shimmering northern lights with his friends.
