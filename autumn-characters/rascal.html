Rascal the Raccoon

Quick-witted and mischevious, Rascal is always up to something - usually involving snacks or shiny things. He's playful rather than mean and fiercely loyal to his friends, even when his ideas cause chaos. Rascal firmly believes every bad idea is worth trying at least once.

Favourite Hobby

Rascal loves searching the forest for shiny little treasures

Favourite Snack

Buttered Sweetcorn
