Flynn the Fox

Clever and calm, Flynn is the quiet thinker of the group. He notices patterns others miss and often helps solve problems with a level head. Flynn doesn't speak much, but when he does, everyone listens.

Favourite Hobby

Flynn loves exploring hidden woodland trails in search of new adventures.
