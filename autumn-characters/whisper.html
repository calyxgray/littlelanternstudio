Whisper the Wolf

Observant and protective, Whisper keeps a watchful eye on everyone around him. He's calm, steady, and deeply caring, even if he doesnt always show it openly. Whisper makes others feel safe just by being nearby.

Favourite Hobby

Whisper loves listening to the woodland breeze and the songs of the birds.
