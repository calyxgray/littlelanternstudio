Hiccup the Hedgehog

Gentle and easily startled, Hiccup often trips over his own feet and apologises to things that don't need it. He's kind-hearted, curious and always trying his best, even when his nerves get the better of him. Hiccup reminds everyone that it's okay to be a little clumsy and awkward.

Favourite Hobby

Hiccup's favourite hobby is collecting tiny, shiny lanterns. He spends hours wandering through the woods, searching for the most beautiful and unique lanterns to add to his collection.
